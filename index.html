Print for me

The "Print for Me" service allows you to easily use the self-service printer. You can upload the documents you want to print and get your own unique printing code. By using this code at the self-service printer, your documents will be printed quickly and efficiently.

How it works

Upload

Upload and set Printing Option for your document and Get your "Print Code"

Found

Found nearest self printer device to you

Print

Enter your "Print Code" on the self printing device and enjoy your documents printed in high quality in seconds.
